Amateur Radio @ HOPE XII

Posted on July 19, 2018 by joe

HOPE XII

Once again there will be a strong Amateur Radio presence at the Hackers on Planet Earth conference in New York City starting this Friday, July 20 through Sunday, July 22.

I will be presenting a workshop on a low cost way to experience Amateur Radio Digital Modes.

More details on all the Amateur Radio activities including a temporary repeater and special event station we will be running.

For what it’s worth,

– Joe, NE2Z
